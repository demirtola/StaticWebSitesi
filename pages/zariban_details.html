Zariban - 2023

While employed at Daz Tech, I collaborated with my colleague Yakup Bağrıaçık on a project tasked with constructing an Unmanned Ground Vehicle (UGV) for JOYCE, a leading company in the field. While JOYCE excelled in crafting the chassis of the UGV, they encountered challenges in developing the control system. To address this, we implemented a sophisticated solution using Pixhawk and integrated it with other components. This resulted in a versatile UGV that could be operated seamlessly using both an RC controller and autonomously.

It took us a dedicated 2-3 weeks to finalize the Pixhawk and motor control circuits for our project. In the end, our efforts paid off as we successfully achieved dual control functionality – seamlessly operating with both an RC controller and autonomously.
