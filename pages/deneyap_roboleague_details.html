Teknofest Deneyap Roboleague

Deneyap Roboleague - 31/08/2023 - 03/09/2023

After finishing our second year of university, my friends and I discovered an exciting opportunity at Teknofest called the "Deneyap Roboleague." We were really enthusiastic about this competition because it matched perfectly with what we were studying. We felt confident in our skills and knowledge, so we took an online exam to show what our team could do. After passing the exam, we eagerly headed to the "Deneyap Roboleague" competition.

The competition lasted four days. On the first day, we explored the Teknofest exhibits and shows. Then, we returned to the competition area, where we were introduced to the challenging parkour course that our robot would need to complete. The organizers explained the rules and gave us the materials needed for our robot.

2th and 3th days

After receiving the materials, three of my friends started building our robot. Another friend worked on creating a special controller for the vehicle, while I handled the programming tasks. By the end of the day, we had successfully assembled the robot, and it communicated perfectly with our controller.

On the third day, we built a robot arm designed to knock down a target. At the same time, we tested our robot for any issues and fixed them carefully. Once everything was working well, we attached the robot arm to our car, completed the programming, and assembled all the parts to finish our car.

Here are some specifications of our car:

  • Microcontroller: Our car is powered by the "Deneyap" Board, which acts as the main microcontroller for precise control and automation.
  • Communication: We used ESP-NOW for communication, ensuring efficient and reliable data exchange between our controller and the car.
  • Control: The controls are very smooth and precise, allowing for accurate movement and operation.
  • Robot Arm: A key feature of our car is the robot arm mounted on the rear, adding to its versatility and functionality.
